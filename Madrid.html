Welcome to Madrid!

Madrid is the capital and most populous city in Spain. It is known for its amazing art, culture, and food.

Art

Madrid is one of Europe's top art capitals, home to famous museums like the Prado and Reina Sofía. The city features works from artists like Goya, Velázquez, and Picasso, blending classic and modern styles.

Culture

Madrid has a vibrant culture filled with festivals, music, and nightlife. From traditional flamenco to modern city life, the culture is energetic and deeply rooted in Spanish traditions.

Food

Madrid is known for its amazing food scene, including tapas, churros, and traditional Spanish dishes. The city offers everything from casual markets to upscale dining experiences.
